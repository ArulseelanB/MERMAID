Changing Themes

The marker stroke should be from the current theme: 
    %%{init:{"theme":"dark"}}%%
    flowchart
    A --> B
  
    %%{init:{"theme":"dark"}}%%
    flowchart LR
    A --> B
    
    flowchart LR
    A -->|marker should be dark brown| B
  
    %%{init:{"theme":"base"}}%%
    flowchart LR
    A -->|marker should be dark brown| B
